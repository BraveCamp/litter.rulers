Tips

Help save our environment!

Buy things in Bulk

Doing this will help with reducing the number of plastic bottles produced. For example, instead of buying plastic bottles of dishwashing liquid each time one runs out you could buy it in bulk. That way you can get a large bottle of dishwashing liquid and pour it into a smaller one.

Reduce, reuse and recycle!

This is a popular solution when helping the environment. Recycling plastic bottles reduces the amount of plastic manufactured. Placing a recycle bin in your home, school, etc. can also help.

Clean Up Clean Up!! Everybody Clean Up!

Joining clean up campaigns at your school and workplace can really make an impact in moving towards beautifying our country. Encouraging others can help, so grab your friends, family and relatives and clean up!

Donate

Raising funds for organizations such as:

  • NEPA (National Environment Planning Agency)
  • JET (Jamaica Environment Trust)
  • EFJ (The Environmental Foundation of Jamaica)

Conserve Water

Conserving electricity and water is also a way of helping the environment. This can be done at home, school and work. Ways to conserve water at home are: using a bucket to wash cars instead of a hose, take showers instead of baths, turn off the tap while brushing teeth and when soaping up in the shower. At school you can: Place ice cream containers under school water fountains and use excess water in the garden and report leaks.

Conserve Electricity

Turn off all lights and fans when not in use, don't use a dryer hang clothes on the line, decide what you want to eat before opening the fridge so you don't leave it open, open your curtains instead of turning on the lights (exposure to natural increases happpiness!), replace your light bulbs with fluorescent or LED bulbs, plug out appliances when not it use, use solar panels.
